Bapers

As part of this team project I was assigned to the design and development of a piece of software. I worked closely with two peers and had the responsibility of identifying what task needed to be done and if necessary delegate that task to a peer. The aim of this assignment was to create a program that allows employees of a hypothetical photography company to view information on their customers/themselves, as well as jobs and tasks that have/haven't been done. It also generates PDF reports of the monthly performance of the company detailing things like how many jobs a certain employee has completed in a month. Managers are authorised to generate backups of their database directly from the system.

The program starts off by taking in the username and password of the hypothetical staff member, these values are then checked against securely stored details in a remote database to see if they match. Once matched, the system will display tabs that the employee is authorised to view.
